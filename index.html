❓ How it works

🌴 Start a trip (one person): name it, pick the country (currency sets itself, ₹ for India), add your name. Then tap the dashed code chip and paste the invite in your group chat.

🎟️ Join (everyone else): open the invite link or enter the 6-letter code, type your name. You'll see the group, total and every expense, live.

✍️ During the trip: anyone adds expenses, even a small chai. Everything updates on all phones instantly (green live dot = connected). Wrong entry? Tap 🗑️. Friend without a phone? Use + person.

🏁 When it ends: tap End trip (ends for the whole group). You get each person's share, who pays whom with the fewest transfers, where the money went, plus trip insights. Tap ✨ Ask AI for tips to spend less in that country.

🗺️ Past Trips tab keeps finished trips, tap any for the full breakdown.
